From the Beginning...

It started with a dream in 1972 when John Fawson and his wife, Jean, sold their produce at the local farmer's market. John and Jean would travel from their humble farm in Santa Paula to Long Beach (a trip of about 90 miles each way)...to sell grapes, dates, avocados and various kinds of citrus fruits. As the time went on, John and Jean started to network with other farmers in the Southern California region.They started hosting their own farmer's markets. The popularity of these events grew and grew, with more locals and tourists who were looking for exceptional and locally grown produce. Eventually this led to John and Jean purchasing their first food truck and in 1995, they started out on their journey to bring their fresh fare to the masses on the coast. In 2015, John Jr. and his wife, Alicia, took over the operations of the business and opened a store-front location in Carlsbad, California that carries produce and blends specialty drinks. The humble food truck has grown to a fleet of 6 which spend their time alternating between a dozen different sites during the week offering fruit cups or specilty blended drinks. Ernesto and Maria have always credited God with their success, and as a way to give back, they vowed to never work on Sundays. To this day Bountiful Foods, LLC continues to remain closed on Sundays so that their employees can spend time with their families and religious observances if they so choose. What started out as a small dream has become a successful business venture which pairs the local farmers, as well as some regional, national and internationl suppliers, with the consumer who is looking for the freshest produce available.
