The Southern California Coast

Located next to the Pacific Ocean, the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego is breathtakingly beautiful. From endless sunsets over the horizons, to miles and miles of sandy white beaches, the coastal area... boasts natural beauty.
With average daily temperatures ranging from 60°F to 75°F, the Southern California beaches are a magnent for locals and tourists alike. Enjoying so many attractions, wonderful dining experiences, unique shopping experiences, and the occasional celebrity sighting, the coastal area of Southern California is a picturesque venutre of health and convenience.
The Mediterranean climate affords the region the ability to cultivate and produce many subtropical fruits including cherimoyas, guavas, avocados, dates, sapotes, citrus fruits and a wide variety of vegetables.
At Bountiful Foods, LLC, we are pleased to partner with many local farmers to bring the freshest produce to the public. By offering unique and convenient offerings from fruit cups, to made-to-order juices, Bountiful Foods, LLC seeks to be the go-to for all of our customers produce needs.
