News Release

Local schools have been given a generous donation of $1 million dollars each by Mr. Hugh L. Jones

Mr. Jones first came to Elk Grove in 1968 as a young salesman for the Torax Company. Upon finding the area very condusive to family life, Mr. Jones decided to stay and raise his family. He worked in various odd jobs until in 1986 he opened his first restaurant along Main Street. As the popularity of Hugh's Cafe spread, another location was opened in Northern Elk Grove. Some time later, in 1999, Mr. Jones started to franchise his Cafes, and now there are over 100 locations nationwide. Mr. Jones retired in 2002 and has continued to help in revitalization projects, especially in the schools. With this sizeable donation, computers will be purchased for all school-aged children, and certain properties will be remodeled to bring them into the 21st century.
