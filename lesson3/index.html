Site Name:

Elk Grove Commerce Chamber

Site Purpose:

The Elk Grove Commerce Chamber site seeks to connect businesses with the community we serve. We know that every community thrives on those who live, work and play there, so we are dedicated to connecting the businesses within the community with those who also call Elk Grove home. This site will provide a brief historical narrative of Elk Grove through the years, from a small farming community to a booming economic center. We want to help the community engage with one another, so we are hosting many community events. This site will also provide a directory of the businesses which are run here. There is so much to offer our community, from colleges to cafes, schools, churches, shopping, athletic events and more.
