Target Audience:

The target audience for the Elk Grove Commerce Chamber are those who live and work in Elk Grove. Elk Grove has established itself as a family community with more than three-quarters of the population owning their own home. 90% of the adults aged 25 years and older have at least graduated from high school with a third of that percentage acquiring a bachelor's degree or higher. With over 90% of households having access to a computer and internet access, this site will be able to help businesses attract customers.

Persona 1:

Kate Green is a 42-year-old white, single mom. She owns a small boutique in an older part of Elk Grove that caters to baby and toddler fashion. She is interested in the Elk Grove Commerce Chamber Website as she seeks to get more business in her store. Because of the location, she doesn't get the foot traffic she would like. Most of her business comes from word-of-mouth. She would like to get her name and business out there by connecting with other businesses and members of her community. She is looking for a different space to rest which will allow her to be in a more desirable location, and help her expand her business. She would also like to find others in her community who have similar interests as her and a place where she can feel comfortable raising her family.

Persona 2:

Anil and Kaeya Patel are a young married couple whose families immigrated from India. They have recently settled in Elk Grove with Kaeya's parents. Anil and Kaeya want to open a resturant which featuring flavors from back home. With a demographic that supports this type of restaurant venture, they are interested in looking for spaces that will allow them to run the restaurant, but they would also like to have a banquet space that can be rented out. Families are essential in their culture, and gathering in families would make a great business venture if they offer a place for families to rent out. They would like to connect with other Indian business owners in the area as well as teach others in their community about their culture.

Scenarios:

  • Where can I find a dance studio for my child?
  • I am trying to sell my house and need to find a reputable realtor.
  • What types of animals can I expect to see at the nature preserve?
  • How many restaurants can cater an event for 300 people?
  • I have friends coming in from out of town and I need some place for them to stay.
